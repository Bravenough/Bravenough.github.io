[Proactive Multi-Intersection Signal Control via Multi-Scale Predictive Learning and Phase-Aware Dynamic Graphs](https://arxiv.org/)

Xipeng Jiao, Chang Li, **Xinrun Wang**

**ICIC 2026** (CCF-C, Oral)
- We propose **MSCoLight**, a proactive multi-intersection traffic signal control framework. It integrates a Direction-Aware Phase Dynamic Graph (DAPGraph), a Multi-Scale Online Traffic Prediction Model (MS-OTPM), and a Spatially Attentive Cooperative Reasoning (SACR) module. MSCoLight reduces waiting rate by **18.19 pp** and travel-time loss by **0.182** over PRLight on the Jinan benchmark.

[SceneSelect: Selective Learning for Trajectory Scene Classification and Expert Scheduling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24514)

**Xinrun Wang**, Deshun Xia, Ke Xu, Weijie Zhu

**ICIC 2026** (CCF-C, Oral Outstanding Award — Top 1%) &nbsp; [\[arXiv\]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24514)
- We propose **SceneSelect**, a novel scene-centric selective learning paradigm for trajectory prediction. It uses unsupervised clustering on geometric/kinematic features to discover scene taxonomy, then dynamically routes inputs to the optimal expert model. Achieves an average **10.5% improvement** over strong baselines on ETH-UCY, SDD, and NBA benchmarks.
